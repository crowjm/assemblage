Must Reads

  • Everybody Hurts: Content for Kindness

    Sara Wachter-Boettcher

    One of my favorite talks ever. It's not enough to keep our users at the center of our work; it's also imperative that we build and make with kindness and empathy. You are not your user.

  • The Gap

    Ira Glass

    As a beginner, you get into a field because you're interested in it, not because you're already good at it. This talk addresses the taste gap and is one I revisit frequently.

  • On Burning Out

    Marc Jenkins

    Burn out is something everyone experiences. Marc talks about his experience and explains some things that helped him overcome his burn out (some of which helped me get through a recent period of burn out).

  • Overwhelmed by Code

    Susan Robertson

    There's always something new to learn in tech. Susan writes about how to find what you want to focus on instead of being overwhelmed with a feeling that you have to learn it all.

  • It's Dangerous to Go Alone: Battling the Invisible Monsters in Tech (video)

    Julie Pagano

    One of the instructors at TIY had all the students watch this video in one of our first weeks of class. Imposter syndrome is real and can be hard to overcome. I watch this talk every time I start feeling like an imposter.
